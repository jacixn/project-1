## Description (4000 characters max)

Biblely is your daily companion for faith, focus, and fitness. Designed for believers who want to grow spiritually while building healthy habits — all in one beautifully crafted app. No subscriptions. No paywalls. Completely free.

BIBLE READING
• Access over 40 Bible translations including KJV, NIV, ESV, and more
• Daily verse to start your morning with inspiration
• Listen to chapters with natural text-to-speech audio
• Highlight, bookmark, and save your favourite verses
• Smart verse search and chapter navigation
• Reading streaks to keep you consistent

PRAYER & DEVOTION
• Write and track personal prayers with timestamps
• Mark prayers as answered and reflect on God's faithfulness
• Community prayer wall to share and support others
• Prayer reminders at times you choose
• Simplified verse explanations to deepen your understanding

SMART TASK MANAGEMENT
• Organise your day with a clean, intuitive todo list
• Smart task prioritisation helps you focus on what matters
• Daily and weekly planning built around your goals
• Earn points and build streaks as you complete tasks

WORKOUT TRACKING
• Choose from dozens of workout templates or create your own
• Log sets, reps, and weights for every exercise
• Track your physique with detailed muscle group breakdowns
• Body composition insights: BMI, body fat, muscle mass, and more
• Personalised balance coaching to keep your training well-rounded

NUTRITION & FUEL
• Scan food photos to instantly estimate calories and macros
• Import photos from your library for quick logging
• Track daily calories, protein, carbs, and fat
• Personalised daily targets based on your goals
• Save favourite meals for one-tap logging
• Body composition insights powered by research-based formulas

SOCIAL & COMMUNITY
• Connect with friends and share your faith journey
• Post updates, encouragements, and prayer requests
• Message friends directly within the app
• See how your community is growing together

BEAUTIFUL DESIGN
• Stunning themes and wallpapers to match your style
• Smooth animations and intuitive navigation
• Dark mode for comfortable evening reading
• Works offline — your data is always available

YOUR DATA, YOUR CONTROL
• All data stored locally on your device
• Optional cloud sync with your account
• Delete your account and all data at any time
• No ads. No tracking. No analytics SDKs.

Whether you're deepening your faith, building better habits, or pursuing a healthier lifestyle, Biblely brings everything together in one place. Start your journey today.
