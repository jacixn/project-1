Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

Biblely, developed and operated by Jason ("we", "our", or "the app") is a faith, fitness, and productivity companion available at biblely.uk. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address, display name, username, name, age, gender, and profile avatar selection. You can choose from preset avatar images or, if your email is verified, upload a custom profile photo (see Section 1.7).

1.2 User-Generated Content

Content you create within the app, including:

  • Prayers and prayer boards (including folders/envelopes, stickers, photos, backgrounds, and custom board layouts)
  • Journal entries and personal notes attached to Bible verses
  • To-do items, task descriptions, and task schedules
  • Saved and highlighted Bible verses, bookmarks, and verse notes
  • Workout logs, workout templates, and workout split plans
  • Nutrition data including food logs and favourite meals
  • Social feed posts and direct messages
  • Custom exercises created by you

1.3 Health and Fitness Data

Workout history, exercise logs (sets, reps, weights), workout split plans (training days, muscle groups, exercise counts), available equipment preferences, nutrition tracking (food logs, calorie and macronutrient data, food text descriptions), and body composition information including height, weight, age, gender, BMI, body fat percentage, muscle mass, visceral fat, body water percentage, and body age. Physique scoring data (individual muscle group scores, balance suggestions, training frequency analysis) is calculated locally from your workout history. This data is used solely to provide personalised fitness and nutrition features within the app.

1.4 Conversation and Chat Data

Messages sent through the Bible Friend chat feature, the Coach (fitness) chat feature, and direct messaging between users. Both chat features support text input and voice input (speech-to-text via your device microphone). Conversation messages sent to external services for personalised responses are described in Section 4.

1.5 Usage Data

App interaction data such as streaks, points, achievement progress, quiz scores, Bible timeline exploration, Bible map visits, Bible character reading progress, and feature usage — used to provide achievements, leaderboards, and personalised experiences.

1.6 Device Information and Push Notification Tokens

We access device information (via Expo Device) solely for push notification delivery. We collect Expo push notification tokens to deliver notifications you have opted into (such as prayer reminders, workout reminders, task reminders, and streak alerts). We do not collect or store device identifiers for tracking purposes.

1.7 Photos and Camera

If you choose to use the camera or photo library, images are processed for profile pictures, food nutritional analysis, gym/physique analysis via the Coach feature, or prayer board customisation. Photos used for food scanning or the Coach feature are sent to Google Gemini for analysis and are not stored on our servers. Photos added to prayer boards are stored locally on your device.

Profile picture uploads: If you are an email-verified user and choose to upload a custom profile photo, the image is sent to Google Gemini for automated content moderation before it is accepted. This scan checks for inappropriate content (nudity, violence, hate symbols, etc.). If the image passes moderation, it is uploaded to Firebase Storage and the download URL is stored in your user profile. If it does not pass, no image is stored and you are informed of the reason. A 24-hour cooldown is applied after a rejected upload to prevent repeated attempts.

1.8 Preferences and Customisation

Your app preferences including selected theme (from 25+ available themes), loading animation choice, Bible translation preference, notification settings, weight unit preference (kg/lbs), and referral status. These are stored locally on your device and synced to the cloud for cross-device access.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and personalise app features (Bible reading, prayer tracking, prayer boards, workouts, nutrition, physique tracking, todos, journal)
  • To sync your data across devices via Firebase and iCloud
  • To send push notifications (prayer reminders, workout reminders, task reminders, streak alerts) that you opt into
  • To generate personalised insights and responses using external services (see Section 4)
  • To suggest workouts based on your muscle split plan, available equipment, and physique scoring data
  • To enable social features (direct messaging, friend connections, leaderboards, social feed)
  • To provide text-to-speech audio playback of Bible passages and chat responses
  • To convert voice input to text for chat features (speech-to-text)
  • To recognise text from images (OCR) for supported features
  • To track achievements, points, and progress across Bible, fitness, and productivity features
  • To link to external exercise tutorial videos on YouTube based on exercise names

3. Data Storage and Retention

3.1 Local Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage, so the app works offline. Food logs older than 90 days and workout history older than 90 days are automatically cleaned from local storage. Exercise data is cached locally for up to 30 days.

3.2 Local Caches

  • Bible cache: Downloaded Bible translation data is cached locally on your device for up to 30 days for offline reading.
  • Text-to-speech audio cache: Generated audio files for Bible passages and chat responses are cached locally on your device for up to 1 year to reduce repeated processing.
  • Exercise database cache: Exercise data is cached locally for up to 30 days.

3.3 Cloud Storage (Firebase)

Your data is synced to Google Firebase (Firestore) servers located in the United States to enable cross-device access and social features. Cloud data is retained for as long as your account exists. When you delete your account, all associated cloud data is permanently removed.

3.4 iCloud Sync

On iOS, your data may be synced via Apple iCloud (CloudKit) if you are signed into iCloud. This is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Images and text are processed and discarded; they are not stored by Google Gemini.

4.5 Google Cloud Text-to-Speech

Used to generate audio for Bible passage reading and chat response playback. Bible text and chat message text are sent to Google Cloud for speech synthesis. Text is processed and discarded after audio generation.

4.6 Google Cloud Speech-to-Text

Used for voice input in chat features (Bible Friend and Coach). When you use the microphone to speak a message, the recorded audio is sent to Google Cloud Speech-to-Text for transcription into text. Audio is processed and discarded after transcription is complete; it is not stored by Google.

Google Cloud STT Data Logging Policy

4.7 OCR.space

Used for text recognition from images. Images are sent for processing and are discarded after recognition is complete.

OCR.space Privacy Policy

4.8 GitHub

Bible translation data, exercise data, and quiz question data are fetched from GitHub servers. Your device connects to GitHub to download these data files. No personal data is sent to GitHub as part of this process.

4.9 Resend

Used for email delivery of verification codes. Your email address is shared with Resend solely for this purpose.

4.10 Expo Push Notification Service

Used for push notification delivery. Your Expo push token and notification content are shared with Expo to deliver notifications.

4.11 YouTube

The app provides links to YouTube for exercise tutorial videos. When you tap a tutorial link, your device opens YouTube (the YouTube app or website) with a search query based on the exercise name. No personal data is sent to YouTube by the app — the link simply opens a YouTube search page. Your interaction with YouTube after tapping the link is governed by YouTube's own policies.

YouTube (Google) Privacy Policy

4.12 Open Food Facts

Used for barcode-based food lookup. When you scan a food barcode, the barcode number is sent to the Open Food Facts API to retrieve nutritional information. No personal data is sent — only the barcode number. Open Food Facts is a free, open-source food products database.

Open Food Facts Terms of Use

5. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence, including:

  • United States: Firebase (Google) servers for cloud storage, authentication, and database services.
  • People's Republic of China: DeepSeek servers for text-based analysis and personalised insights.

Where data is transferred internationally, we rely on the data protection practices of our service providers, including their compliance with applicable data protection frameworks. We select service providers that maintain appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data. For transfers to DeepSeek in China, we limit the data shared to what is necessary for the specific feature and do not send account credentials or authentication tokens.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Data is only shared with the third-party services listed in Section 4, solely to provide app functionality. We do not sell your personal information as defined under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) or any other applicable privacy law.

7. Analytics and Tracking

We do not use any analytics or tracking SDKs. We do not track you across apps or websites. No advertising identifiers are collected.

8. Your Rights

8.1 General Rights (All Users)

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app at any time.
  • Rectification: You can correct or update your personal information through the app's profile and settings screens.
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data from Settings > Delete Account. This permanently removes your data from Firebase and your device.
  • Portability: Your data is stored locally on your device and accessible through standard device backup mechanisms.
  • Restriction of processing: You can request that we limit how we process your personal data by contacting us at the email below.
  • Objection: You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data. Contact us at the email below to exercise this right.

8.2 Rights for Users in the European Economic Area (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Legal basis for processing: We process your personal data on the following legal bases:
    • Legitimate interest: For core app functionality such as syncing data, delivering notifications, and providing Bible reading features.
    • Consent: For optional features such as sending data to DeepSeek or Google Gemini for personalised insights, food analysis, workout suggestions, and coaching. You can withdraw consent at any time by stopping use of the relevant feature.
    • Contract performance: To provide the services you have requested when you create an account.
  • Right to lodge a complaint: You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority if you believe your data has been processed unlawfully.
  • Data transfers: When your data is transferred outside the EEA (see Section 5), we rely on appropriate safeguards as described in that section.

8.3 Rights for California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know: You have the right to request that we disclose the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you, the categories of sources from which it was collected, the business purpose for collecting it, and the categories of third parties with whom we share it.
  • Right to delete: You have the right to request deletion of your personal information. You can do this directly in the app via Settings > Delete Account, or by contacting us.
  • Right to opt-out of sale: We do not sell your personal information. We have never sold personal information and have no plans to do so.
  • Non-discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights.

Categories of personal information we collect: Identifiers (name, email, username), health and fitness data (body composition, nutrition logs, workout data, physique scores, equipment preferences), user-generated content (prayers, prayer boards, messages, tasks, journal entries), device information (push notification tokens), and photos (when you choose to use the camera or photo library).

"Do Not Sell My Personal Information": Biblely does not sell, and has never sold, the personal information of its users to any third party for monetary or other valuable consideration.

9. Children's Privacy

Biblely is not directed at children under 12. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 12. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 12, we will promptly delete that data from our servers and local storage. If you believe a child under 12 has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately at biblelyios@gmail.com so we can take appropriate action.

10. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including Firebase Authentication, encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), and secure password hashing. All data transmitted to third-party services is sent over encrypted connections. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
