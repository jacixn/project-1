Fuel. Wisdom in eating.

Macros without the misery.

AI vision. Barcode scan. Actual tracking.

Most calorie apps require manual logging. Biblely's Fuel tab uses on-device AI vision and barcode scanning to make logging frictionless. Pour milk, snap a photo, done. Your daily targets adjust to your goals.

  • Scan Food. Take a photo. Get instant nutrition info via Claude.
  • Import Photo. Choose food photo from your library. Same AI estimation.
  • Scan Barcode. Tap, scan. Open Food Facts product database.
  • Enter Manually. Type food name or calories. Quick entry mode.
  • Favorite Meals. Re-add common foods in one tap. Build your shortlist.
  • Daily targets. Calories, protein, carbs, fat. Calibrated to your weight goal.
  • Auto-recalibration. Lose or gain weight on the scale. Targets update automatically.
  • Nutrition profile. Activity level, goal, body type. Set once, used everywhere.
