Privacy · Built into the foundation

Your faith is not training data.

End-to-end private. Always.

Biblely runs on Firebase + Apple iCloud. Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit, scoped to your account. No selling. No ads. No tracking pixels. Email verification gates AI features to keep abuse out.

  • Server-side rate limiting. Atomic Firestore counters keyed to verified UID. Bypass-proof.
  • Subcollection storage. Per-item docs scale to unlimited verses, workouts, prayers without 1 MiB caps.
  • iCloud sync. Native Apple sync as backup. Your data follows you across devices.
  • Email verification required. AI features locked behind verified accounts to prevent spam.
  • Open architecture. Privacy policy + terms in plain English. No dark patterns.
  • Free forever. No subscriptions, no in-app purchases, no ads. Period.
